Make an Impact with Hunter Strategy.

Deploying secure cloud solutions across public and private industries is an increasingly important service, with huge real-world ramifications. Our partners rely on us to guide them, and none of that would be possible without our incredible team. When you become a part of that team, you can rest assured that your talents will be put to good use. We value fearless curiosity and the ongoing pursuit of expanding deep technical expertise amongst our team of technical leaders, leading to the delivery of uniquely effective solutions. We love automation and the notion that working yourself out of a job equals success.

Our Virtual Reality

Hunter Strategy is headquartered in our nation’s capital, although we know that culture doesn’t have a zip code. Founded in 2015 as a virtual-first company, we were ahead of the curve in the remote-first movement, and we have always embraced the different perspectives of team members across the US.

The Benefits of working with Hunter Strategy.

Competitive Pay

Compensation is determined by talent and skillset, not by zip code. Merit-based increases and bonuses are available to incentivize exceptional contributions.

Comprehensive Health, Dental, Vision, Disability & Life Insurance

100% of the premium of our Nationwide medical plan is covered by the company for full-time employees.

Retirement Benefits (401k) with Employer Match

Reward your future self by participating in our 401k. There’s a 90-day waiting period and then you’re fully vested. The company will match up to 4%.

Transparent & Open Communication with Leadership

We empower our leaders to clear the path for their team members and empower our team members to clear the path for their leaders. Every employee has influence and support.

Professional Development

Employees are encouraged to become who they want to be. Our professional development program provides reimbursement for training and certification expenses.

Continuous SWAG

Stylish, seasonal SWAG is thoughtfully planned, packaged, and delivered to each employee via Hunter Supply Drops.

Paid Holidays & PTO

Full-Time employees enjoy 11 paid Holidays and accrue Paid Time Off to take advantage of throughout the year.

Interactive Events

Team-building and celebratory events range from ziplining to Virtual Wine Tastings and our Annual Hunter Games competition. Individual and team challenges at these events almost always come with a prize.

Internships @ Hunter Strategy

Cyber Security Track

Work with industry leading Cyber Security SMEs to develop real-world cyber threat analysis skills, understand and apply practical Risk Management Frameworks, and learn how to establish a secure cloud environment. This track is 12 weeks in length and focuses on security operations, vulnerability assessments, and penetration testing, as well as best practices for identity management.

DevOps Engineering Track

Contribute to special projects involving the use of JavaScript, Docker, Kubernetes, Ansible, Terraform, and more. This track is 12 weeks in length and provides interns the opportunity to build on and expand full-stack development skills as well as contribute to our GovCloud well-architected framework.
